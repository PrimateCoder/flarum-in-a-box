The [**Flamoji**](https://discuss.flarum.org/d/39130-new-flamoji-emoji-picker-extension-for-flarum) extension adds a visual emoji picker to your posts. Try it out:

1. Start writing a reply or new discussion
2. Click the **smiley face icon** 😊 in the text editor toolbar
3. Browse or search for emoji
4. Click to insert!
