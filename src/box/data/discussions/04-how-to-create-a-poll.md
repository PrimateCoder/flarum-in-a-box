The **Polls** extension is enabled! Here is how to use it:

1. Click **Start a Discussion**
2. Write your question in the title and body
3. Click the **poll icon** in the toolbar (bar chart icon)
4. Add your poll options
5. Set whether users can change their vote
6. Post your discussion!

Polls are a great way to gather community feedback and make decisions together. Try creating one now!
